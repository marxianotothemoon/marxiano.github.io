Transaction Success Checklist

At this point in the worldwide development of crypto, transactions can be confusing. Don't worry, we're here to help! If you can't seem to get a successful transaction, go through this checklist while you execute your transaction and before you click "Swap", make sure your transaction meets each of these criteria for transaction success.

Make sure you have copied the correct MARXIANO contract address

Set slippage to 12% in your exchange's settings on the swap page

Don't try to trade more than 5 000 000 000 000 MARXIANO per transaction

Make sure you haven't changed the gas fees preset by your wallet

Make sure you're swapping Smart Chain BNB and not regular BNB

Make sure you have the correct exchange URL
